# 门户系统

当前门户不是 Vue Portal，而是 KJDP/KUI 页面框架。

## 主要入口

- `apps/opp/index.html`
- `apps/opp/pages/src/index.js`
- `apps/opp/operator/pages/src/index.js`
- `apps/opp/manager/pages/src/index.js`
- `apps/opp/review/pages/src/index.js`
- `apps/opp/settlement/pages/src/index.js`

## 框架视图

- `frame/views/content-view.js`：内容区、菜单页面打开、iframe/页面容器。
- `frame/views/tabs-view.js`：标签页和菜单页签。
- `basic/ready.js`：登录后缓存和公共能力初始化。
